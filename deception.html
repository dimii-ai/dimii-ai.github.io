Why Deception?

AI thrives on data to be effective. Without exposure to your threats, AI-driven security can become counterproductive— flooding your team with non-critical, out-of-context alerts that drain productivity, cause alert fatigue, and ultimately compromise your security.

We built Dimii's deception platform to provide your AI-driven security with a rich learning environment.

Imagine having an expert AI system that understands your operations—one that can explain every security event in context, flag real threats over noise, and evolve with your environment.

It all starts with our next-generation deception platform—where AI observes attackers in action, learns from red-team exercises, and even runs its own simulated attacks to uncover blind spots. This dynamic intelligence becomes the foundation for stronger defenses, faster response, and a truly proactive security posture.

With Dimii, you can easily design, deploy, scale, and manage a flexible arsenal of next-generation deception platforms—on demand, across cloud or on-prem environments. These intelligent decoys obscure your attack surface, redirecting malicious actors into safe, controlled traps.

Every interaction is a goldmine. We capture and analyze attacker behavior down to the finest detail—profiling threats, identifying new tactics, and delivering high-fidelity insights you can act on.

Our platform also lets you configure live, interactive responses to attackers—helping you test hypotheses, trigger automated defenses, or even expose deeper threat paths. This dynamic engagement gives your team the intelligence edge: precise, contextual, and constantly evolving with the threat landscape.
